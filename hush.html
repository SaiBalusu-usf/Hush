Listen & adapt

The automatic version: it uses your mic for about five seconds to find where the snore sits, then shapes the masking sound to cover it and switches the mic off — nothing is recorded or sent, it all stays on your device. The in-app preview usually blocks mic access; if the button says so, open this page in its own browser tab, or just use Match the snore above, which needs no mic.

Getting the most out of it: set your headphones to full noise cancelling (not ambient) so the steady low drone is gone, then let this cover the irregular snore on top. Keep the volume as low as it can go while still working. For long study sessions, keep this tab open and in the foreground — phone browsers pause audio when the screen locks. The mic in “Listen & adapt” is blocked inside this embedded view; use “Match the snore” for the same effect with no mic, or open the page in its own browser tab to enable the mic.
